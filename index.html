Bihu celebration at SHIKSHA+ campus

Dhemaji, Assam, April 12, 2024 – In a vibrant celebration of Assamese culture, Shiksha Plus institute recently hosted a joyous Bihu festival on its campus in Dhemaji, Assam. The event brought together teachers and students alike, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie. Amidst colorful decorations and traditional music, the highlight of the festivities was the spirited performances by the students, who enthralled the audience with their energetic Bihu dances and captivating cultural performances.

From traditional Bihu dances to mesmerizing cultural performances, the event served as a platform for students to express their creativity and pride in Assamese heritage. As the beats of the dhol and the rhythms of folk songs echoed through the campus, the celebration served as a reminder of the rich cultural tapestry that binds the community together.
